GEAR: Immersive AR/VR Learning

Bring your lessons to life with interactive 3D content directly inside Moodle.

🚀 Overview

GEAR is a powerful Moodle activity module that bridges the gap between traditional e-learning and immersive 3D technology. It allows educators to effortlessly upload, manage, and present 3D models (glTF/GLB) in a fully interactive spatial environment. Whether you are teaching anatomy, engineering, or art, GEAR provides the tools to make your content truly 3D-native.

✨ Key Features

  • 🎮 Full 3D Interaction: Students can rotate, zoom, and pan around complex models with smooth orbital controls and physics-based navigation.
  • 📍 Interactive Hotspots: Add multimedia annotations (text, images, links) directly onto any point of the 3D model to guide the learning process.
  • 🧪 Spatial Quizzes: Embed assessment points within the 3D scene. Results are automatically synced with the Moodle Gradebook.
  • 🥽 VR & AR Ready: One-click support for immersive headsets (WebXR) and AR devices, providing a true sense of scale and presence.

🛠 How it Works

  1. Upload: Simply drag and drop your .GLB or .GLTF models.
  2. Annotate: Use the built-in editor to place hotspots and information points.
  3. Engage: Students enter the scene, explore the model, and complete integrated quizzes.

📱 Why Choose GEAR?

Unlike external 3D players, GEAR is fully integrated into Moodle. It respects your privacy settings, uses Moodle's internal file system, and works seamlessly across Desktop and Mobile (Moodle App). It's designed for scalability, performance, and accessibility.
